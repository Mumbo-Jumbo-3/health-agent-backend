inhibiting the growth of pathogens, it can also help our immune system, bones, address
unbound iron and help with bile metabolism among other things.

**Best time to consume:** Theoretically, there’s no “best time” to consume it.
Anecdotally, most people respond to it better if they take it later in the day (evening/night).

**Usage length:** Given the fact that it can increase the number of red blood cells, i would not
consume it for more than 3-4 weeks at a time.
