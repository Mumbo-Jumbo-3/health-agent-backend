**Phase I: Modification of AFB1** 

AFB1 is oxidized in the liver to form AFB1-8,9-epoxide with CYP1A2 and CYP3A4 being the the primary enzymes involved (CYP2A6 and CYP2B6 also contribute but to a lesser extent).
