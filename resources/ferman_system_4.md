**Suggestion number 16: Take care of your adrenal glands.** 

These two small triangle-shaped endocrine glands that sit on top of the kidneys are quite important. 

They are broken down in the inner part (the adrenal medulla) and the outer region (the adrenal cortex, which is broken down to the zona glomerulosa, zona fasciculata and zona reticularis).
